There is a lot to do on these shores, and if you plan your trip right, you will have plenty of time to check out some of the standout attractions of Curaçao including Mambo Beach Boulevard which has some of the best restaurants, bars, entertainment, and shopping.

Mambo Beach itself is known for great water sports, lots of shade, great amenities and everything you will need for the perfect beach day. Mambo Beach is also known as Sea Aquarium Beach and it is one of Bahama is beautiful manmade beaches.

There is lots to do on and around this beach including spending some time out on the volleyball court, strolling the boardwalk, and even visiting the nearby Sea Aquarium.
